Introducing Our Exclusive New Dishes

A Fusion of Tradition and Innovation

Discover our newly launched menu items, each crafted to bring the authentic flavors of India to your table. From comforting classics to bold, flavorful dishes, our new arrivals showcase the best of Indian cuisine.

Delight in the richness of our Raajma, slow-cooked in a fragrant tomato-based gravy, paired perfectly with basmati rice. For a South Indian twist, try our crispy and golden Dosa, served with a variety of chutneys and sambar that will tantalize your taste buds.

If you're craving something hearty, dive into our aromatic Hyderabadi Biryani, a royal dish bursting with layers of spices and tender basmati rice. And for a street-food favorite, savor our indulgent Chole Bhature fluffy, deep-fried bread served with a spiced chickpea curry.
